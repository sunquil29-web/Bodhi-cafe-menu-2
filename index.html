✅ Live & Ready
Direct WhatsApp Ordering is Active
When a customer places an order, a beautifully formatted WhatsApp message is automatically sent to +91 80163 06629. No API setup needed — it opens WhatsApp instantly with the order details pre-filled.
How it works
Customer places order → WhatsApp opens
1. Customer browses the menu & adds items to cart.
2. They tap "View Order", enter their table number.
3. Tap "Send Order via WhatsApp" — their phone opens WhatsApp with the complete order pre-filled.
4. They press Send — the order lands on your phone instantly. 🪷
Step 01 · For Full API Integration
Create a Meta Business Account
Go to business.facebook.com and create a free Meta Business account using your café's Facebook page or email. This is required for the official WhatsApp Business API.
Step 02
Apply for WhatsApp Business API
Visit developers.facebook.com/docs/whatsapp → Click "Get Started" → Add a WhatsApp product. You'll need a verified phone number and business documents.
Step 03 · Recommended
Choose a BSP (No-Code Option)
Use a Business Solution Provider like Interakt, AiSensy, Wati, or Zoko — ₹799–₹2,499/month, no coding needed. Sign up at interakt.ai or aisensy.com.
Step 04 · Custom Dev
Set Up Your Webhook
When a customer places an order, the app can send a POST to your backend which forwards to WhatsApp via:

POST https://graph.facebook.com/v18.0/{phone-id}/messages
